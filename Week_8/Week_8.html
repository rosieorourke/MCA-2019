Task 2.2: What Are The Advantages of a Time-Frequency Analysis over a Wave-Form Analysis?

A time-frequency analysis is a more realistic approach at analysing a piece of music as the user can identify what feature is happening at a specific point in the music. A waveform-based analysis only provides a vague representation of the music, however a spectogram allows editors to pinpoint specific events in the piece. The piece I am analysing (feel like drugs) shows more sparse sections in the piece which I can identify with a certain time in the song, therefore I already know at 1:36:00 the music is going to be softer and less instruments are going to be present.

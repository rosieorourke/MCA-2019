Task 1: Metadata Schema

Below are the metadata elements that I would extract from a thousand mei files due to their importance in describing both the technical and non-technical elements of each piece.
  • Title: The Title of the Piece of Music
  • Composer: The Person(s) Who Composed the Piece
  • Date: The Date the Piece was Published
  • Place of Publish: The Location of Which the Piece Was Published In
  • Genre: The Genre(s) of the Piece
  • Encoder: The Person(s) Who Encoded the Piece
